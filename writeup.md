# Design Rationale

## Approach

The system is designed as a deterministic decision tree to guide structured reflection.

---

## Question Design

Questions are simple, realistic, and based on human behavior.

---

## Axis Design

Axis 1: Control (Internal vs External)
Axis 2: Contribution vs Entitlement
Axis 3: Self vs Others

---

## Logic

Each answer leads to a fixed branch. No randomness is used.

---

## Improvements

More depth, better reflections, and UI can be added in future.
